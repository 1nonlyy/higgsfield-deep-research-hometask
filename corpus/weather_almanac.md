# Midwest spring frost dates and hail frequency (unofficial almanac)

This unofficial almanac compiles volunteer-reported last-frost dates across counties from Iowa through southern Wisconsin, spanning 1998–2023. Median last frost hovers near April 28 for low-lying urban heat islands but shifts to May 10 for rural frost pockets; microclimates along river valleys dominate variance.

Hail frequency peaks in June when strong surface moisture and steep lapse rates support supercells. Insurance loss summaries correlate with stone sizes above two centimeters; composite radar trends since 2010 show modest increases in mesoscale convective system tracks but mixed significance once population bias is removed.

The almanac includes planting windows for sweet corn, snap peas, and winter squash, none of which connect to spacecraft operations. Keywords such as **storm**, **radar**, and **climate** sometimes collide with Earth science mission pages; this file exists to diversify token statistics for BM25 experiments without asserting expertise in numerical weather prediction.
